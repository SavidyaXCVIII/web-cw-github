Stephenie Meyer

Stephenie Meyer( born December 24, 1973) is an American novelist and film producer, best known for her vampire romance series Twilight. The Twilight novels have sold over 100 million copies, Meyer was the bestselling author of 2008 and 2009 in the US, having sold over 29 million books in 2008. Twilight was the best-selling book of 2008 in the US
